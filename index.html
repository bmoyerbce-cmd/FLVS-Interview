Role Snapshot for the Interviewer

Use this opening summary
The Call Center operates 365 days a year, providing customer support for FLVS users with technical and non-technical inquiries. Responsibilities include responding to phone calls, assisting customers with self-service support tickets, performing basic troubleshooting, and delivering excellent customer service. This temporary position offers flexible scheduling, with part-time and full-time opportunities ranging from 20-40 hours per week. Shifts are available during operating hours from Monday through Friday, 6:00 AM-8:00 PM CT, and Saturday, 8:00 AM-4:30 PM CT. Employment is contingent upon successfully passing a Level 2 background check and drug screening.
